# Privacy Policy for Lumixa

**Effective Date**: December 20, 2025
**Developer**: NextFyn

## Information We Collect
Lumixa does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users.

## Data Storage
- Wallpaper favorites are stored locally on your device only
- No data is sent to external servers
- Downloaded wallpapers are saved to your device gallery

## Third-Party Services
- We use Pexels API to fetch wallpapers (subject to Pexels Privacy Policy)
- We use SoundJay for audio content (subject to SoundJay Terms)

## Permissions
- **Storage Permission**: Required to save wallpapers to your device
- **Internet Permission**: Required to download wallpapers

## Data Security
All user preferences and favorites are stored locally on your device and are not accessible to us or third parties.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, contact: nxtfyn@gmail.com

## Changes
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app.

*This policy complies with Google Play Store requirements.*
